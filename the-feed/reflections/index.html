What do you usually use the internet for the most?

I usually use the internet for shopping, fashion news, research, school, and social media. I'm always looking for new clothes to buy literally every chance I get. But, when I'm not buying clothes, I'm looking at the most recent runway collections from different fashion brands. And normally, I'm using it for homework, research, and social media.

My screenshot reflections

Through the process of Glance back taking screenshots of my face, all the screenshots happened when I first opened my Chrome browser, but never when I was searching for something on the browser. So, whenever it took a screenshot of me, I always just typed something I was thinking at that moment. For instance, my first screenshot was of when I cut my hair and wasn't used to it, so I reflected on how I regretted cutting my hair off. And for most of them, I just reflected on how I felt in that moment. I didn't get the chance to explore the internet while having Glance back take a photo of me.

Touching some grass

Life online in contrast to in real life are two completely different experience. It's one thing to see something online a say to yourself "Wow, that's so beautiful! I wish I could be there!" and actually getting the full experience of life itself.
